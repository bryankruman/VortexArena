Global mechanics — cross-release changes

These settings apply to all weapons and fundamentally change how combat feels. The v0.8.0 row in particular contains three of the most impactful changes in the game's history.

Setting Release Before After Effect
g_balance_armor_blockpercent 0.1pre→0.5.0 0.70 0.60 Major. Armor absorption reduced from 70% to 60% at v0.5.0. Later restored to 70% at v0.8.0 — see below.
g_balance_selfdamagepercent 0.1pre→0.5.0 0.65 0.75 Self-damage penalty increased at v0.5.0 — then reduced again at v0.6.0 (see row below).
g_projectiles_newton_style_2 factors 0.1pre→0.5.0 min 0.7 / max 5.0 min 0.8 / max 1.5 Relative-velocity envelope tightened. Style 2 (relative) still active at v0.5; absolute (style 0) not until v0.8.0.
g_balance_selfdamagepercent 0.5→0.6 0.75 0.65 Reverted back to the v0.1.0preview value. Less splash self-punishment — encourages aggressive close-range play.
g_balance_weaponswitchdelay (global) 0.5→0.6 0.15 0.20 Slower global weapon switching. Later replaced by per-weapon switchdelay_drop/raise at v0.7.
g_throughfloor_damage 0.5→0.6 0.40 0.75 Splash damage through floors nearly doubled — boosted floor-level explosive effectiveness.
g_throughfloor_force 0.5→0.6 0.70 0.75 Force through floors also increased.
per-weapon switchdelay_drop/raise 0.6→0.7 global 0.20 per-weapon Global delay replaced. Most weapons: 0.2/0.2. Vortex (Nex): 0.3/0.3 (slower to equip/unequip).
g_projectiles_newton_style 0.7→0.8.0 2 (relative+corrected) 0 (absolute) Major. Style 2 added player velocity to projectiles (rockets felt faster when running forward). Style 0 = absolute Quake-style: rocket speed is always constant regardless of player velocity.
g_projectiles_damage 0.7→0.8.0 2 (destructible) −2 (invincible) Major. Projectiles can no longer be destroyed mid-flight. Rockets, grenades, and orbs survive all incoming fire. Dramatically changes the incentive to shoot at incoming projectiles.
g_balance_armor_blockpercent 0.7→0.8.0 0.60 0.70 Major. Armor absorbs 70% of damage (up from 60%). Significantly extends effective health for armored players; rewards item control more.
g_projectiles_keep_owner 0.8.0→0.8.1 0 1 Projectiles no longer collide with their owner. Blaster/rocket jumping no longer requires moving away from the explosion.
g_projectiles_interact vote 29 (2025) 2 1 Projectiles no longer push each other on contact. Paired with the Devastator damageforcescale change below.
g_projectiles_spread_style post-0.8.6 7 4 (Gaussian) Spread distribution changed from uniform to Gaussian — random-spread weapons now cluster more toward center, reducing outlier shots.

Armor block reduced; self-damage raised; starting health lowered; newton envelope tightened

Nine months of active development between the first public release and v0.5.0. The two most impactful global changes are the armor block reduction (0.70→0.60, making armor less effective) and the self-damage increase (0.65→0.75, punishing splash more). Both would later partially reverse — self-damage at v0.6.0, armor at v0.8.0. Per-weapon stats also changed across this period but a full commit-by-commit per-weapon diff has not been traced — see open questions below.

xonotic-v0.8.0  // weapons branch reloaded
Weapon renames; file split; three fundamental mechanics changes

The largest single restructure in the game's balance history. The monolithic balanceXonotic.cfg was split into balance-xonotic.cfg (global settings) and bal-wep-xonotic.cfg (all per-weapon stats). Six weapons were renamed. Three mechanics changes fundamentally shifted how combat plays. Arc was added as a new weapon. Overkill-mutator weapons were added. The charge mechanic was introduced on Vortex.

Three foundational mechanics changes (1) Absolute projectile velocity (g_projectiles_newton_style 2→0): Rockets no longer inherit player momentum — a rocket fired while moving at 600 u/s has the same speed as one fired while standing still. This standardizes range estimation across all movement speeds.
(2) Invincible projectiles (g_projectiles_damage 2→−2): Projectiles can no longer be destroyed mid-flight. No more shooting down rockets in transit.
(3) Higher armor blocking (g_balance_armor_blockpercent 0.6→0.7): Armor absorbs 70% of incoming damage. With 100 armor, an 80 dmg rocket hits for 24 health damage instead of 32 — a 25% effective health increase.

Formal balance votes (post-0.8.6)

Post-release balance changes go through a community vote. Only changes that pass a supermajority are committed to the default preset. Numbers are sequential across all votes, not just the ones that affect the default preset.

Vote Date Change
Vote 25 2023-07-11 MachineGun: remove mandatory reload. reload_ammo 60 → 0. The reload was seen as an accessibility and feel issue — it interrupted combat flow without adding meaningful decision-making. Passed via council vote.
Vote 29 2025-07-12 Reduce projectile physics interactions. g_projectiles_interact 2 → 1 (projectiles no longer push each other) and g_balance_devastator_damageforcescale 1 → 0 (Devastator no longer pushes entities on direct hit). Rationale: rocket physics were creating unpredictable interactions and were frequently exploited.
Vote 30 2025-08-30 Blaster: faster switch and animation. primary_animtime 0.2 → 0.1 and switchdelay_drop/raise 0.2 → 0.1. Makes the blaster-jump-to-weapon combo more viable in rapid play without changing any damage values.
